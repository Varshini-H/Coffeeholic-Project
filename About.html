About Us



Coffeeholic offers a unique coffee house environment unlike any other in TamilNadu. We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee,we are a place where you can sit down and enjoy that made-up cup of coffee.If you need to work, we have a seating area created specifically for you.
If you need to rest, we have a soft-seating area in front of a stone fire place that is perfect for your weary mind. We offer a delicious variety of coffee from Safehouse Coffee made by our professionally trained baristas.
We have everything from classic coffee to our house made specialty beverages. All of our sauces & syrups are made in-house with all natural ingredients (no chemicals or preservatives) ensuring you the highest quality in taste & health.
You can complete your coffee with one of our delicious sweet treats made by our very own baker.
We look forward to serving you at Coffeeholic!
